About
Sovereign OS LLC

An independent research and engineering company studying how complex systems — human and artificial — behave under sustained pressure. We build governance infrastructure to make that behavior visible, measurable, and governable.

Self-funded. Founder-owned. Chiang Mai, Thailand.

Truth over comfort

SIM95 doesn't tell you what you want to hear. The Generation Gap paper doesn't spare vendors. Our instruments measure what is — not what's convenient.

Accuracy over speed

Deterministic scoring, not inference. Pre-registered protocols, not post-hoc narratives. We filed 286 patent claims before launching a single product. If it isn't precise, it doesn't ship.

Why "Sovereign OS"

An operating system governs everything running on top of it. It doesn't do the work — it determines what the work is allowed to do.

"Sovereign" means self-governing. Not dependent on external authority for structural integrity.

SIM95 makes a human's operating system visible. King Sango makes an AI's operating system governable. You cannot govern what you cannot see, and pressure is what makes the architecture visible.
