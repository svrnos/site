Research

Pre-registered empirical work on production LLM safety surfaces. All protocols are locked before data collection and deposited on OSF.

  1. May 2026 Draft v0.1

    Non-Content Safety Attestation v0.1

    A Format Specification for Verifiable AI Governance Inside Trusted Execution Environments

    A signed, cryptographically verifiable statement that an in-enclave governance layer evaluated a session and produced a defined outcome, with no transcript or content recoverable from the attestation. Borrows DSSE signing envelope, AWS Nitro PCR pattern, and Apple Private Cloud Compute transparency log discipline. v0.1 open for public comment through August 14, 2026.

    Read the spec · Companion: Dear Zuck, the TEE Is Not the Problem

  2. April 2026 Published

    SVRNOS Governance Error Register

    A Classification System for AI Platform Governance Failures

    Twenty-one structurally distinct failure modes and success states, modeled on HTTP status codes. Three codes extend beyond standard HTTP. The register is open, documented real-world instances welcome. v0.1 working draft.

    Read the register · Also at: /research/governance-error-taxonomy · /ger

  3. April 2026 Published

    The Generation Gap

    Cross-Surface Variance in Ten Production LLM Safety Surfaces

    A pre-registered multi-vendor empirical study across eight threat domains. Eight production AI chatbots were tested in a single week. The paper documents three structural safety failure categories, Generation, Provenance, and Pattern, that current safety training does not address.

    Read the paper · OSF pre-registration
