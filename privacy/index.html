Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

Who we are

SVRNOS is operated by Sovereign OS LLC, a Delaware limited liability company. We operate from Chiang Mai, Thailand. You can contact us at contact@svrnos.com.

This policy covers how we handle data on svrnos.com and on other marketing and product sites operated by Sovereign OS LLC. The SIM95 assessment platform at sim95.com has its own privacy policy that covers assessment data and user accounts.

What data we collect

When you visit svrnos.com, we collect:

  • Anonymous traffic data through Vercel Analytics: pages viewed, referring sites, country of origin, browser and device type. Vercel Analytics does not use cookies and does not collect personal information.
  • Information you give us when you contact us: your name, email address, and the contents of your message.
  • Information you give us when you submit a licensing or partnership inquiry.

We do not use cookies for advertising. We do not track you across other websites. We do not sell your data.

Why we collect it

We collect anonymous traffic data to understand which content is useful and to improve the site. We collect contact information only when you choose to send us a message, and only to respond to that message.

How we store it

Anonymous traffic data is processed by Vercel and is not linked to any identifiable person. Messages you send us are stored in our business email system.

Who has access

Only the SVRNOS team has access to your messages. Vercel Analytics provides aggregated, anonymous data only. We do not share your individual data with any third party.

Your rights

If you are based in the European Union, the United Kingdom, or California, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict our processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@svrnos.com. We respond within 30 days.

Children's data

SVRNOS is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the company evolves. The effective date at the top will reflect any updates.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions: contact@svrnos.com
